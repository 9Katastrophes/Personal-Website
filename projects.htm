Projects

Magicats

Magicats is an RPG game that takes place in a fantasy world where people worship cats for their cuteness. The player, an ordinary person, is suddenly transported to this strange world and embarks on an adventure to find a way home.

Future Librarians

A game for non-gamers that takes place in a future where books and trees no longer exist. The player can go around and talk to NPCs to get a better idea of what the world is like.
